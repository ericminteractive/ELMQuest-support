FAQ

Q: I lost progress. Can it be restored?
A: The game is saved locally so unfortunately not. This was in part to limit data collection, need for a server, and privacy purposes.

Q: The game feels hard.
A: Defeats are part of progression in ELM Quest. Upgrade town bonuses and adapt your build to push farther each run.

Q: I found a bug.
A: Please email steps to reproduce it, plus your device + iOS version.
